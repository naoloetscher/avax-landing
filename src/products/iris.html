Our Technology

Iris Investigation allows fast and accurate identification of individuals in images, videos and documents. Furthermore, it empowers analysts to focus on the relevant, more narrowed evidence pool, saving you precious time and resources.  Since we understand the importance of data security as well as privacy, we have implemented advanced security protocols to protect data from unauthorized access. 

Problems with existing investigation software:

  • Violence or criminal activities requiring intensive image analysis
  • Capacity constraints: law enforcement unable to review the full extent of data evidence
  • Analysis of cumbersome data material required, always with time constraints
  • Reliable and accurate results are paramount to avoid false positives

Our solution

With Iris Investigation the analysis of terabytes of data occurs in an instant, returning potential face matches with industry leading accuracy and reliability. In the industry-recognised accuracy benchmark ‘Labeled Faces in the Wild’, we outperform even  Google. This allows analysts and investigators to focus on the relevant, narrowed evidence pool.

Unlike most of our competitors, our privacy by design approach enables responsible and compliant use of AI-models for facial recognition technology. Reap the following benefits by using Iris Investigation for your data analysis:

  • Fast and accurate identification of suspects, missing persons or other person of interest in large data sets
  • Meeting the highest reliability standards, without any racial bias
  • Thanks to our AI models developed exclusively in-house and in Switzerland, compliance with any data privacy requirements
  • Enabling your analysts to make better, more informed decisions

Identification of individuals

Our technology is used to quickly and accurately identify individuals in images and videos.

How to use:

In case of criminal investigations, fraud investigations or missing persons our Investigation technology can be used to identify individuals in images, videos or in footage from security cameras in compliance with data protection laws.
