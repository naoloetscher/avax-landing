Face ID match

Use Case:

Client onboarding needs to occur seamlessly and satisfy ever tighter regulation. Particularly the identification process performed by a human is often time-consuming, prone to human error, and lacks scalability.

Our solution:

Face verification in real time for client onboarding with Gemini. Thanks to our highly reliable facial recognition algorithm, you can verify the conformity of official documents with selfies or real-time faces in an instant. The process can occur through any browser-based application or API-calls live on the computer.

Text Analysis

Use Case:

Starting with big data and getting value out of it can be a daunting task. Appropriate know-how is required to properly organize, analyze, and implement data in order to get the most value out of it.

Our solution:

Gain insights from large data sets with Heliospan. Heliospan allows financial organizations to structure and organize large sets of data. It utilizes advanced text analysis, semantic search and data mining technologies to structure and organize large sets of data, enabling identification of potential risks and opportunities.
