Products

Sentinel

Access Control

Sentinel Access Control enables real-time biometric access control based on facial recognition technology. This empowers reliable access management with granting or denying access to buildings, events or security areas within milliseconds.

Iris

Investigation

Iris Investigation accelerates your digital data analysis with matching faces from different data sources. Our specially trained AI model offers the highest level of accuracy and reliability.

Heliospan

Heliospan includes state-of-the-art natural language processing (NLP) technologies for data mining, text analysis and semantic search. The technology accurately understands the meaning of the user's request and the document pool.

Gemini

Gemini allows you to verify official identification documents with the actual face of individuals in real time within a few seconds. This accelerates your account or contract openings, and at the same time provides protection against spoofing attacks.
